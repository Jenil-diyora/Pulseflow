Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Pulse Tap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Pulse Tap mobile application. This app is designed for users of all age groups.

1. Information We Collect

Account & Authentication Information:
If you choose to sign in using Google, we may collect basic profile details such as your name, email address, and profile picture. This helps us save your progress and allow you to continue playing across multiple devices.

Game Progress Data:
We store game-related information such as scores, levels, experience points (XP), achievements, and unlocked themes. This data is used to provide features like leaderboards and progress tracking.

Device & Technical Information:
We may collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system version, and app performance data to improve stability and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain game functionality
  • To sync game progress across devices
  • To display leaderboards and in-game rankings
  • To improve performance, security, and user experience
  • To display ads in a responsible manner

3. Third-Party Services

Pulse Tap uses trusted third-party services that may collect information as part of their functionality:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, cloud storage, and leaderboard data.
  • Google AdMob: Used to show advertisements. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user settings, age, and region.

4. Children & Family-Friendly Use

Pulse Tap is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without appropriate safeguards. If you believe that a child has provided personal data unintentionally, please contact us so we can remove it.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data using industry-standard security practices. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Deletion & User Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. Once deleted, your profile and game progress will be permanently removed from our servers, including leaderboard records.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, along with an updated revision date.
