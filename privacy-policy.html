Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Pulse Tap ("we", "our", or "us") provides this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Authentication: If you sign in with Google, we collect your basic profile information (name, email, and profile picture) to sync your game progress across devices.

Game Data: We store your high scores, levels, XP, and unlocked themes on our servers (Firebase) to provide leaderboard functionality.

Device Information: We may collect basic device info (OS version) to help troubleshoot technical issues.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and database (Leaderboards).
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

3. Data Deletion

Users can request data deletion by contacting us through our support channels. Deleting your account will remove your profile and scores from our global leaderboard.

4. Children's Privacy

Pulse Tap is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
